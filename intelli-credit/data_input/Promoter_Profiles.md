## 1. Key Promoters Overview
**1.1. Shri Brij Bhushan Singal (Chairman)**
*   **Background:** Over 40 years of experience in the steel sector. Founded the company in the 1980s. Highly technically proficient in steel manufacturing (Secondary processing).
*   **Net Worth Declared:** ₹ 1,250 Crore (as of FY16).
*   **CIBIL Score (Consumer):** 745
*   **Litigation Check:** Associated with two civil suits regarding land acquisition in Odisha, currently sub-judice. No recorded criminal convictions.

**1.2. Shri Neeraj Singal (Vice Chairman & Managing Director)**
*   **Background:** Son of Brij Bhushan Singal. Handles procurement, finance, and banking relations. Responsible for the aggressive capacity expansion in Angul (Phase I, II, and III).
*   **Net Worth Declared:** ₹ 890 Crore.
*   **CIBIL Score (Consumer):** 710
*   **Reputation / Market Intel:** Known for being highly aggressive in negotiations. Heavy reliance on debt-funded brownfield expansions.

*   **Company CIBIL Rank (CMR):** CMR-6 (High Risk). Down from CMR-4 in the previous year.
*   **CRILC Status:** Tagged as *SMA-1 (Special Mention Account 1)* by PNB and UCO Bank in October 2017, indicating principal or interest payment overdue between 31-60 days.

## 3. Qualitative Insights / "Field Notes" (Credit Analyst Comments)
*   **Project Execution Risk:** The Angul plant expansion is suffering severe cost overruns and delays (approx. 18 months behind schedule). The promoters continually request additional working capital tied to this uncompleted unit.
*   **Group Company Complexities:** There are over 60 privately held unlisted subsidiaries connected to the Singal family (e.g., Bhushan Energy, Bhushan Capital, various holding private limited companies). The corporate structure is highly opaque.
*   **Key Man Risk:** The organization is entirely promoter-driven. Professional management team exists but holds highly restricted financial authority. All banking relationships rest solely with Mr. Neeraj Singal.

# CONFIDENTIAL: LOAN SANCTION MEMORANDUM
**Lender:** Consortium of Lenders (Lead: State Bank of India - Corporate Accounts Group)
**Borrower:** Bhushan Steel Limited (BSL)
**Date of Sanction:** October 12, 2016
**Reference Number:** SBI/CAG/BSL/2016-17/4491
**Sanctioning Authority:** Management Committee of the Board (MCB)

---

## 1. Credit Facilities Sanctioned
The consortium is pleased to sanction the following credit facilities aggregating to **INR 5,500 Crores** under the Joint Lenders' Forum (JLF) Corrective Action Plan (CAP):

| Facility Type | Sub-Limit | Amount (INR Cr) | Tenor / Validity | Pricing (Interest) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fund Based: Term Loan (TL-V)** | Fresh Capex (Angul Phase 3) | 3,000 | 8 Years (incl. 2 yr moratorium) | Base Rate + 350 bps (Currently 12.80% p.a.) |
| **Fund Based: Working Capital (CC)** | Cash Credit | 1,500 | 12 Months (Renewable) | Base Rate + 275 bps (Currently 12.05% p.a.) |
| **Non-Fund Based (NFB)** | Letter of Credit (LC) / Bank Guarantee (BG) | 1,000 | 12 Months (Renewable) | Commission at 1.5% p.a. + out of pocket expenses |

---

## 2. Security and Collateral
The above facilities are secured by a strict Pari-Passu charge among consortium members:
*   **Primary Security:** First pari-passu charge on all current assets of the company (present and future), including raw materials, stock-in-process, finished goods, and receivables.
*   **Collateral Security:** First pari-passu charge on the fixed assets of the company, specifically the integrated steel plant located at Meramandali, Angul District, Odisha.
*   **Personal Guarantees:** Unconditional and irrevocable Personal Guarantees of Shri Brij Bhushan Singal (Chairman) and Shri Neeraj Singal (Vice-Chairman & MD).
*   **Corporate Guarantees:** Corporate Guarantee of M/s Bhushan Energy Limited.

---

## 3. Key Financial Covenants
The Borrower must strictly maintain the following ratios, tested quarterly based on statutory auditor certificates:
1.  **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR):** Shall not fall below **1.20x** at any point.
2.  **Total Outside Liabilities to Tangible Net Worth (TOL/TNW):** Shall not exceed **3.0x**.
3.  **Current Ratio:** Minimum of **1.33x** to be maintained.
4.  **Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR):** Minimum of **2.0x**.

---

## 4. Special Pre-Disbursement Conditions
*   Borrower must submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from existing lenders outside the consortium before availing the fresh Term Loan.
*   Borrower to route a minimum of 60% of total sales realizations through the Trust and Retention Account (TRA) maintained with the Lead Bank.
*   No dividend shall be declared if any event of default (including non-payment of interest) is persisting.
*   Borrower shall not undertake any new major capital expenditure (exceeding INR 100 Crores) without prior written approval from the Lead Bank.
